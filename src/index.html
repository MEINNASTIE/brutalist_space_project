About

This project was possible thanks to our wonderful source providers: NASA Open API, Open Notify from Space and Space X Open API.

Who are we? A singular entity that decided to not abide the logics of usual web design. "We" strive to change and shape the Web according to the modern standards and imply all that is beautiful in this world into a compact beating developing heart. Walk with "us" to find more interesting happenings in the future!

"We" do not possess any rights, thus you won't notice any copyrights floating around. Instead, lets focus on the time we have left on this planet to reminiscence without doubts, withouts worries...Let yourself free.

Why Brutalism

Brutalism in web design is a design style that is characterized by its unconventional, raw, and unpolished appearance. It often intentionally rejects the principles of traditional web design, such as aesthetically pleasing layouts and user-friendly interfaces.

This webpage serves as a playground to such. Elements are playfully placed around the webpage to invoke deliberate search for purpose. Diagrams were taken to honor a small tribute to Apollo mission.

All diagrams provided were an open free source taken from NASA archives
